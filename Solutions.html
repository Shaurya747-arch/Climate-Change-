Solutions to Climate Change

1. Use Renewable Energy

Energy from the sun and wind does not pollute the air. Using solar panels and windmills can reduce harmful gases.

2. Plant More Trees

Trees absorb carbon dioxide and give us oxygen. Planting more trees helps fight climate change.

3. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Recycling waste and reusing materials reduces pollution and saves natural resources.

4. Save Electricity and Water

Turn off lights when not needed and avoid wasting water. Small actions by many people can make a big difference.
