Climate Change Awareness

Climate change is one of the most serious problems the world is facing today. It refers to long-term changes in temperature and weather patterns, mainly caused by human activities such as burning fossil fuels, cutting down forests, and pollution.

The Earth is getting warmer, glaciers are melting, sea levels are rising, and natural disasters like floods, heatwaves, and wildfires are becoming more frequent. If we do not act now, the future of our planet and all living beings is at risk.

This website explains the problems, solutions, and how climate action is linked to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
