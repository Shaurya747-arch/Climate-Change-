Problems Caused by Climate Change

1. Rising Temperatures and Heatwaves

The Earth is getting hotter every year. Many places now face very high temperatures. Heatwaves can cause health problems for people and damage crops and animals.

2. Melting Glaciers and Rising Sea Levels

Ice in the polar regions and mountains is melting. This causes sea levels to rise. Coastal cities may get flooded in the future.

3. Floods and Heavy Rain

Some places receive too much rain which leads to floods. Floods destroy homes, roads, and farms and put many lives in danger.

5. Loss of Wildlife

Many plants and animals cannot survive these climate changes. They lose their homes and some species may become extinct.
